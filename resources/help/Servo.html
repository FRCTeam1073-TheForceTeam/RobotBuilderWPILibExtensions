Servo

What is it?

A servo is a device whose rotational position is controlled by PWM signal from the robot. The servos included in the kit of parts are usually low power and have 170 degrees of rotation.

Properties

Output Module (Digital)
The Digital Sidecar on the CRIO that the output is plugged into.
Output Port (PWM)
The PWM port on the digital sidecar that the output is plugged into.
