Digital Output

What is it?

An output that can be in one of two states: high or low. This type of output is generic and can be used for many purposes when interacting with other sensors, such as an ultrasonic sensor.

Properties

Output Module (Digital)
The Digital Sidecar on the CRIO that this digital output is plugged into.
Output Port (Digital)
The DIO Port on the digital sidecar that this digital output is plugged into.
