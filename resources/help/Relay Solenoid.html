Relay-based Solenoid

What is it?

A solenoid that is controlled by a relay. It controls a pneumatic cylinder to create linear motion.

Properties

Output Module (Digital)
The Digital Sidecar on the CRIO that this solenoid is plugged into.
Output Port (Relay)
The relay port on the digital sidecar that this solenoid is plugged into.
