Compressor

What is it?

The compressor keeps the pneumatics of the system pressurized up until the digital switch detects that there is too much pressure.

Properties

Pressure Switch Module (Digital)
The Digital Sidecar on the CRIO that the pressure switch is plugged into.
Pressure Switch Port (Digital)
The DIO Port on the digital sidecar that pressure switch is plugged into.
Spike Module (Digital)
The Digital Sidecar on the CRIO that the spike that controls the compressor is plugged into.
Spike Port (Relay)
The relay port on the digital sidecar that the that controls the compressor is plugged into.
